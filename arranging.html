Dr David Cashman

PhD, MMus, GradCertAdEd, BMus

Dr David Cashman is a Sydney-based pianist, educator, researcher, and advocate. His area of research is live music performance and industry, particularly in regional areas and the performance practice thereof. He is an adjunct Associate Professor of Contemporary Music at Southern Cross University, and a founding member of the Regional Music Research Group.
